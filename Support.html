# Support - AeroPax Live Welcome to AeroPax Live support! We're here to help you get the most out of your flight tracking experience. ## Contact Information For any questions, issues, or feedback, please reach out to us: **Email:** admin@av8r.com.au **Response Time:** We typically respond within 48 hours during business days. ## Frequently Asked Questions ### General Questions **Q: What is AeroPax Live?** A: AeroPax Live is a flight tracking application designed to help you record and monitor your flights in real-time. **Q: Which devices are supported?** A: AeroPax Live is available for iOS devices running iOS 17.0 or later. **Q: Is AeroPax Live free to use?** A: Please check the App Store for current pricing information. ### Getting Started **Q: How do I start tracking a flight?** A: Open the app, grant location permissions when prompted, and use the flight tracking interface to begin recording your flight. **Q: Why does the app need location permissions?** A: Location permissions are required to accurately track your flight path and provide real-time position information. ### Flight Records **Q: Where are my flight records stored?** A: All flight records are stored securely on your device using Apple's Swift Data framework. **Q: Can I export my flight history?** A: Check the Flight History section in the app for export options. **Q: How do I delete a flight record?** A: Navigate to your flight history, select the flight you want to delete, and use the delete option. ### Troubleshooting **Q: The app isn't tracking my location accurately. What should I do?** A: 1. Ensure you've granted location permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services 2. Make sure "While Using the App" or "Always" is selected for AeroPax Live 3. Check that your device has a clear view of the sky for GPS signal 4. Restart the app if needed **Q: My flight data isn't showing up. What's wrong?** A: 1. Ensure the flight was properly saved 2. Check your internet connection if using online features 3. Try restarting the app 4. If the issue persists, contact support **Q: The app crashes when I open it. How can I fix this?** A: 1. Force quit the app and reopen it 2. Restart your device 3. Check for app updates in the App Store 4. If problems continue, please contact us with details about when the crash occurs **Q: I'm experiencing a white screen issue. What should I do?** A: Please refer to our [White Screen Fix Guide](WHITE_SCREEN_FIX.md) for detailed troubleshooting steps. ### Privacy & Data **Q: Is my data secure?** A: Yes, all your flight data is stored locally on your device with industry-standard security measures. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details. **Q: Do you share my data with third parties?** A: We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for more information. **Q: How do I delete all my data?** A: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app from your device. ### Sensors & Hardware **Q: What sensors does the app use?** A: AeroPax Live may use GPS, accelerometer, gyroscope, and barometer to provide accurate flight tracking. For more details, see our [Internal Sensors Setup Guide](INTERNAL_SENSORS_SETUP.md). **Q: Does the app work in airplane mode?** A: GPS tracking can work in airplane mode, but some features requiring internet connectivity may be limited. ## Feature Requests We love hearing from our users!
